✗ One dot beside two empty rings reads as “1 out of 3”

Dots are awards, not ratings. Most buildings will have none, and that's the default — a visited entry with no dots is perfectly complete. Dots are reserved for the rare few that earn one, two, or three. Never render empty placeholders: a 1-dot award is one dot, not one filled plus two hollow. The absence of dots carries meaning; filling them with outlines dilutes it.
