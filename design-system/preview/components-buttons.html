Primary — black fill, default
Accent — lime, use sparingly

Reserve lime for moments that matter. Landing-page hero CTA, the single key action on an onboarding screen, a celebratory confirmation. At most one lime button per screen. On every other surface, use btn-primary (black) — the lime is most effective when it's rare.
